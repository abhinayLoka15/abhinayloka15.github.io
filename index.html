Privacy Policy for Loka15 Studios Platform

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

1. Introduction and Legal Basis

Welcome to Loka15 Studios ("we", "us", or "our"). We operate the Loka15 Studios Platform, an AI-powered content creation service.

We are committed to processing your personal data in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 of India.

  • Data Fiduciary: Loka15 Studios
  • Data Principal: You, the user
  • Consent: By using our service, you provide informed consent.
